About Me

I'm a software engineer from Kalamazoo, Michigan. I graduated with an masters degree in telecommunications from Michigan State University, and am currently working at DataRobot on the Platform engineering team.

Outside of work I am an avid maker. I build or retrofit CNC machines, but lately I've been enjoying building robots. I also enjoy writing web apps with a preference for APIs built using Python and Molten with VueJS frontends. IoT devices are also a thing I do; If you haven't already, check out MicroPython and its fork Circuit Python.

Prior to my work at DataRobot I was an Analytics Solutions Architect at Kelloggs, the food company. That's right, I kept the Cheez-its on the shelf for six years, but I wanted to write software instead of directing others.

Feel free to get in touch via email or hit me up on Keybase!
